QR PrimeScan – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06/Feb/2025

Last Updated: 06/Feb/2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using QR PrimeScan. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data when you use our macOS application.

2. No Data Collection

QR PrimeScan is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

3. How QR PrimeScan Works

  • The app operates locally on your Mac and does not transmit any data.
  • All QR code scans are processed directly on your device.
  • The History Log is saved locally and remains under your full control.

4. No Third-Party Services

QR PrimeScan does not integrate with third-party services, analytics tools, or tracking systems. No information is shared with external companies.

5. Security & User Control

Since the app functions offline, your scanned QR codes and history remain private. You can delete or export your history at any time.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this document.
